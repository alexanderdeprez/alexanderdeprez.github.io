Memoires consists of a combination of images from different series. I use the images to discover myself, who I am, where I belong. My wife, my family, my friends, they are all characters in my work. I photograph them as they are in the moment, mercilessly, without concealing the situation. With my work I'm trying to defy the social norms that keep us from being ourselves. Having no shame in it and trying to be as honest as possible.
