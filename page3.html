Alexander Deprez (° 1995) was born in Kortrijk. Now he studies photography at Sint-Lucas Ghent. Through his work he allows the viewer to take a look at his private life, his view of the world, the intimate relationship with his wife. His photos are voyeuristic and often leave you with an uncomfortable feeling.

The analogue images he makes with his pocket camera are raw, direct and in-your-face. His work tries to defy the social norms that keep people from being themselves.
