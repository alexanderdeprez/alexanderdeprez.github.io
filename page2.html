The series 'Nur Für Verrückte' consists of images made during my stay in Berlin. The title describes the general theme of the series. "We" the peripheral figures of society. We who are labeled as deranged by everyone and everyone because we are not in the list. Because we are proud of who we are and the obscure practices we practice. The drugs, the sex, the parties. The way we live and how we deal with gender and sexuality. We are the "mad men" and we feel no shame about this
