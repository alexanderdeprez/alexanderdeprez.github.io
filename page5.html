“To love! To surrender absolutely, to prostrate oneself before the
divine image, to die a thousand imaginary deaths, to annihilate
every trace of self, to find the whole universe embodied an
enshrined in the living image of another! Adolescent, we say. Rot!
This is the germ of the future life, the seed which we hide away,
which we bury deep within us, which we smother and stifle and do
our utmost to destroy as we advance from one experience to
another and flutter and flounder and lose our way.”

- Henry Miller, Sexus
