NN

The series "NN" is about the intimacy and the relationship with my wife.
She is twenty-four years older and has ME.
I photograph her on an almost daily basis and try to be honest
picture of her and our moments together.
Often when she is just awake, tired, feasting or sick. I want to show her
as she reveals herself at those moments.
The letters NN, her initials, do not just refer to me anymore
her as a person, but also to the period since I met her
and the impact she has on my life.
